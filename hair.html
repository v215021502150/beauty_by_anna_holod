Our Hair Services

From precision cuts to complex colour techniques — all delivered with care and expertise.

Haircuts & Styling

Precision cuts for all hair lengths and types. Whether you want a clean trim, a structured bob, layers or a complete restyle, Ekaterina will shape your hair to enhance your features and suit your lifestyle.

Balayage & Highlights

Balayage creates a natural, sun-kissed gradient that grows out beautifully. Classic foil highlights give brighter, more structured results. Both techniques are personalised to your base colour, skin tone and desired look — natural or bold.

Full Hair Colouring

Single-process all-over colour for covering grey, changing your shade or refreshing your current colour. We use professional products chosen for your hair type to deliver rich, even and lasting results.

Colour Correction

Unhappy with a result from another salon? Brassiness, patchiness or an unwanted tone can all be corrected. Ekaterina assesses your hair's condition and history, then creates a safe, step-by-step plan to achieve the colour you actually want.

Toning & Glossing

Keep colour fresh and vibrant between full sessions. Toning neutralises unwanted warmth and adds dimension, while glossing treatments add mirror-like shine and improve the overall texture and health of the hair.

Restorative Treatments

Damaged, dry or over-processed hair needs targeted care. We offer deep conditioning and restorative treatments designed to rebuild strength, restore moisture and improve manageability — returning shine and softness to your hair.
