Nail Care You Can Trust in Limassol

At Beauty by Anna Holod we believe that beautiful nails are the result of precision, quality products and strict hygiene — not shortcuts. Our nail specialists take the time to understand your preferences, nail condition and lifestyle before every session.

Whether you are looking for a classic nude manicure, an expressive nail art design, a long-lasting gel polish or a thorough hardware pedicure — we have everything you need under one roof in central Limassol on Arch. Makarios III Avenue.

Our clients describe the experience as professional, fast and beautiful — you leave the studio with nails that look impeccable and stay that way for weeks.

Nail Services We Offer

From everyday gel manicure to detailed nail art — find the service that suits you.

Classic Manicure

Shaping, cuticle care and polish application. The foundation of healthy-looking nails. Perfect for those who prefer a natural finish or regular nail polish over gel.

Gel Polish Manicure

Long-lasting colour that resists chipping for 3–4 weeks. Available in hundreds of shades — from sheer nudes to vivid neons. Cured under UV/LED lamp for an instant dry finish.

Hardware Pedicure

Deep callus removal, cuticle care and nail shaping using professional hardware tools. Longer-lasting results than classic pedicure, no water soaking required — more hygienic and precise.

Nail Art & Design

Custom nail art tailored to your style — geometric patterns, French tips, ombre, hand-painted florals or minimalist accents. Discuss your idea and we will bring it to life.

Removal & Correction

Safe gel polish or extension removal without damage to the natural nail plate. We also correct grown-out gel manicure and fix broken nails before applying fresh polish.

Nail Strengthening

Ideal for thin, brittle or damaged nails. We use professional strengthening bases and top coats to protect the nail plate and help it grow healthy and strong over time.
