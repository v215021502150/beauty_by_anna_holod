Booking FAQ

The easiest way is to book online via the button above. You can also reach us via Instagram DM (@beauty_by_anna_holod), WhatsApp or by calling +357 95 167 831. We recommend booking 1–2 days in advance for regular services, and earlier for complex hair colour appointments.

Walk-ins are welcome if there is availability, but we cannot guarantee a slot. To avoid disappointment, we strongly recommend booking in advance — especially for massage, hair colour or body treatment sessions.

For manicure, pedicure, waxing and brow services — 1 to 2 days is usually enough. For massage, body treatments and hair colour sessions, we recommend booking 3–5 days ahead as these are in high demand.

We accept cash and bank card payments. Please contact us directly for up-to-date payment options.
