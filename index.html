Your Beauty Destination in Limassol

Beauty by Anna Holod is a professional multi-specialist beauty salon located in the heart of Limassol on Arch. Makarios III Avenue. We bring together a team of certified specialists under one roof so you can take care of your nails, skin, hair and body without travelling across the city.

Whether you are looking for a long-lasting manicure, a professional anti-cellulite massage, smooth skin after a waxing session, or a stunning new hair colour from a skilled colourist — we have the right specialist for you.
