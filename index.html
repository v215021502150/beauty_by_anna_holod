Your Beauty Destination in Limassol

Beauty by Anna Holod is a professional multi-specialist beauty studio located in the heart of Limassol on Arch. Makarios III Avenue. We bring together a team of certified specialists under one roof so you can take care of your nails, skin, hair and body without travelling across the city.

Finding a beauty salon in Limassol that genuinely combines quality, hygiene, professional results and a welcoming atmosphere is not always easy — especially in a city that is growing as fast as Limassol is. That is exactly the gap that Beauty by Anna Holod was created to fill.

Whether you are looking for a long-lasting manicure, a professional anti-cellulite massage, smooth skin after a waxing session, or a stunning new hair colour from a skilled colourist — we have the right specialist for you. All in one address, all in a clean and comfortable space. We serve both Russian-speaking and English-speaking clients, and our central location on Makarios III Avenue makes us one of the most conveniently situated beauty studios in Limassol.
