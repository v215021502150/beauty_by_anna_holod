About Beauty by Anna Holod

A professional beauty salon in Limassol — our story, our team and what we stand for.

Our Story

Beauty by Anna Holod was born from a simple but important idea: that women in Limassol deserve a beauty salon where they can get everything done in one place — without compromising on quality, hygiene or atmosphere.

Anna Holod — the founder and lead specialist — built this salon around the belief that beauty care should feel personal, results-oriented and genuinely comfortable. She personally performs all massage and body treatment sessions, bringing her certification, experience and care to every client who comes through the door.

Located on Arch. Makarios III Avenue 161 — one of the most central streets in Limassol — Beauty by Anna Holod has grown into a trusted destination for both the Russian-speaking community and international residents of Cyprus. Our clients come for one service and stay for many, because everything they need is right here.

What We Stand For

Real Results

We do not promise miracles — we deliver measurable outcomes. Whether it is reduced swelling after a body wrap course, a long-lasting manicure, or a hair colour that turns heads, our clients see and feel the difference.

Cleanliness First

Every tool is sterilised, every workspace is clean, and we use disposable materials wherever required. Hygiene is not an afterthought at Beauty by Anna Holod — it is the standard we hold ourselves to every single day.

Genuine Warmth

We believe that how you feel during a treatment matters as much as the result. Our team is friendly, attentive and genuinely interested in each client — which is exactly why so many people become regulars after their first visit.

Conveniently Located in Limassol

Our salon is on Arch. Makarios III Avenue 161 — one of the most central and accessible streets in Limassol. Whether you are coming from the city centre, Kolonaki, or the seafront area, we are easy to reach by car or on foot. Street parking is available nearby.
