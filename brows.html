Professional Brow Correction in Limassol

Well-shaped eyebrows are one of the most powerful tools in a beauty routine. A precise arch, clean lines and the right density can completely transform your face — lifting your features, making your eyes appear larger and framing your natural expression. At Beauty by Anna Holod in Limassol, our brow specialists take a personalised approach, designing every brow shape to complement your unique facial structure.

We believe in natural beauty above all. Our brow services focus on correction, care and enhancement — no microblading, no tattooing, no permanent procedures. Just expert hands, the right technique, and brows that look polished and effortless.

Whether you want to maintain a clean shape you already love, redesign overgrown brows, or add definition with tinting — we will help you achieve the look that feels like you.

Our Eyebrow Services

Precise shaping, natural colour and lasting definition — all in one studio.

Brow Correction & Shaping

We remove unwanted hair using wax or threading to create a clean, precise shape tailored to your face. The result is an instant lift and a more polished look. Recommended every 3–4 weeks.

Brow Tinting

Brow tinting adds colour, fills in sparse areas and makes your brows appear fuller and more defined — without any makeup. Results last 3–5 weeks. Can be combined with correction in a single session.

Brow Design & Reshape

If your brows have grown out, lost their shape or were previously over-plucked, our specialist will design a new shape that flatters your features. We work gradually to grow and guide the hair in the right direction.

Who Benefits from Brow Correction?

  • Anyone who wants well-groomed, natural-looking brows without tattooing or semi-permanent procedures
  • Clients with overgrown or undefined brows who need a complete reshape
  • Those with sparse or light-coloured brows who want more definition without makeup
  • Busy professionals who want a groomed appearance with minimal daily effort
  • Anyone wanting to refresh their look before a special event, photoshoot or holiday

What Results Can You Expect?

The difference a professional brow correction makes is immediate and remarkable. After a single session you will notice that your face looks more symmetrical, your eyes appear more open and your overall appearance is more defined. Clean, well-shaped brows require less daily makeup and give you a polished, ready appearance even on no-makeup days.

With tinting, the effect is amplified — brows appear fuller, younger-looking and perfectly framed, lasting three to five weeks before a touch-up is needed. Many clients tell us that after their first professional brow session, they finally understand the transformative power of properly shaped brows.
