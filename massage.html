Types of Massage We Offer

Each massage type is designed for a specific goal — choose based on what your body needs right now.

Relaxation Massage

Full-body relaxation using gentle, flowing strokes that release muscle tension, calm the nervous system and restore a sense of wellbeing. The effect is felt immediately — even after a single session.

Anti-Cellulite Massage

Targeted deep-tissue massage that breaks down fatty deposits, improves circulation and visibly smooths skin texture. A course of 10 sessions delivers the most effective results for problem areas like thighs, buttocks and abdomen.

Lymphatic Drainage Massage

Light, rhythmic movements that stimulate the lymphatic system, reduce fluid retention and relieve puffiness. Especially popular in Cyprus's warm climate where swelling in legs and face is a common concern. Visible reduction in just one session.

Back & Neck Massage

Focused therapy for the back, neck and shoulder area — the most common zones of tension for those who work at desks or use computers. Relieves pain, improves posture and releases chronic muscle knots.

Full-Body Massage

A comprehensive session covering all major muscle groups. Combines relaxation and therapeutic techniques adapted to your current physical condition and priorities. The most popular choice for first-time visitors.

Massage + Body Wrap Combo

Combine massage with a body wrap for maximum results. Massage opens the pores and improves circulation, while the wrap delivers active ingredients directly to the skin. Together they accelerate inch loss and skin firming.
